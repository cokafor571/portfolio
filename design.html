Design Process

Web Design

The design process begins with a pen and a paper, a basic sketch of core ideas that will become a mock-up. A mockup represents a flat graphic file that illustrates the visual direction of the project, page layouts, navigation, incorporation of company brand identities, and ultimately a feel of what the final product will be. At this stage feedback and alteration is essential in order to produce an end product that captures the initial vision.

Web Development

Once a visual layout is finalized, development of a custom website can begin using common web coding standards(Html, CSS, Javascript, PHP, jQuery). Once development has completeed a prototype website will be provided that has all functionality of the intended final product. After the prototype has been approved the website is ready to launch to the world.
